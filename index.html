Frequently Asked Questions

Trip style

Which style of trip is right for me?

Our three styles of travel Basix, Original and Comfort cover a whole gamut of travel experiences. To find out which one has You written all over it, visit our trip styles page.

Are trips physically demanding?

Want to lie in a hammock and not move until cocktail hour? Weve got a trip for that. Want to power up the side of mountain at high altitude? Weve also got a trip for that. To determine what type of trip suits you best, each of our trips comes with a Physical Rating to let you know how physically demanding it is… or isnt.
